Happy Birthday!

It's been 5 years already since we met.

Things were way different back then. We were both happy and believed that we had
a bright future ahead of us. We were dreamers, already planning lots of things like
our jobs, where we'll live, where we'll go, and when we'll get married.
Things are different now.

We're both lost, we don't know what to do and where to go. We're left with no motivation.
We've both lost the will to live and are desperately searching what it means to live.

Whenever you're birthday comes, you treat it as another day. It's been that way for a long time and it will probably stay that way. But for me it's different.

On my bad days, what keeps me going is knowing that I still have you.
We may not be 100% happy but even being 1% happy because of you is enough to live for.
When I'm with you, I feel like I'm right at home.
So, for me, your birthday is one of the days I'll always look forward to.
It may just be a +1 in your life but it's that +1 that gave me you.

Happy 24th Birthday, I love you!


-- James
